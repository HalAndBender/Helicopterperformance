About
This site is free to use for anyone. It does not collect any data.
It is intended to help helicopter pilots and operators quickly assess and compare the performance limitations of their helicopters near seal level.

Instructions
The calculations require the input of various environmental and operational conditions, such as wind, QNH, height above sea level, etc. The range of the allowed input values are displayed in the input fields themselves. Reasonably accurate calculations can only be achieved within the ranges displayed. The calculations are based on the information provided in the Rotorcraft Flying Manuals from the helicopter manufacturer.

Disclaimer
Results provided here must be validated by the Pilot in Command. No guarantee for the correctness or accuracy of the results is given.

Under development
The site was launched in April 2021 and is currently under development. In the future we hope to be able to offer performance calculations for a variety of helicopters and conditions.
